Who I am

My name is Ludwig Schreier and this is my website with information about my professional background and activities.

I currently work as a full-time Software Requirements Manager as part of an E-Mobility AUTOSAR and Functional Safety development team.

I'm a guy who likes to dive into technical or scientific issues, while understanding projects, processes and business at the same time.

What I do

I'm an engineer and manager with many years experience in the Automotive and Semiconductor industry. I speak German and English and this is an overview of my knowledge areas:

Software Engineering

I understand bits and bytes from high abstraction, application level to low embedded regime. My knowledge is comprised of architecture and internals of AUTOSAR, RTOS, BSW, CAN, Diagnostics, Unix/Linux, etc.

Security and Safety

I understand security and safety design, engineering and process challenges, e.g. ASIL B or D (ISO26262). I took part in audits and worked closely on architectural and process issues.

Consulting

I coach Waterfall, Agile, V-Model, Change Control, CI, Requirements and Project Management. This also includes researching standards, norms, specifications e.g. SPICE, MISRA, ISO, IEC, IEEE

Embedded Systems

I'm interested in computer microarchitectures and embedded systems. I worked with Automotive ECUs, Xilinx FPGAs, NVIDIA GPUs, microcontroller etc.

Systems Engineering

My knowledge includes electronics systems like Automotive ADAS Radar devices (SRR, ACC), Lidar, inductive charging system and thin-film solar cells.

Leadership and Excellence

I strive to be a continuous learner and enjoy pushing boundaries. I model processes, use cases, requirements, enterprise architecture and business development.
